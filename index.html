Is BizNeighbors right for you?

You should join BizNeighbors:

🏪

If you already own a physical store.

From a coffee shop to a yoga studio, a dog grooming salon to a CPA office — every business can benefit from creating cross-promos with local partners.

✅

If you have a business website, a Google or Yelp business page that can be verified.

Every member is verified — so you get relevant discussions and reliable advice from real business owners.

💡

If you provide services tailored to business owners and are willing to share your expertise.

Advertising of services is not allowed on the platform, however you are welcome to build your reputation by providing valuable advice to the community.

⚠️ Please note, we are not accepting virtual business owners or non-business owners at this time.
