BizNeighbors Happy Hour for Local Business Owners

BizNeighbors is excited to host our first Happy Hour for local business owners in Berkeley area! If you own, lease, or operate a physical storefront or commercial space, we'd love to meet you.

A special thank you to ACME Bar & Co for generously hosting us in their side courtyard.

Snacks and refreshments will be provided. Join us for an informal meet-and-greet with fellow business owners in your neighborhood:

  • Meet the people behind the businesses on your block
  • Share what you're working on and discover opportunities to support one another
  • Exchange resources, ideas, and local insights
  • Explore collaborations that help our business community thrive
  • Apply to become a BizNeighbors Founding Member and receive FREE lifetime membership

Whether you've been in business for years or just opened your doors, this is a great opportunity to build connections with your neighboring businesses in Berkeley neighborhood.

We look forward to seeing you there!
